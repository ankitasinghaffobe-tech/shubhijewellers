Our Story

A Legacy of Craftsmanship

Shubhi Jewellers was born from a vision of bringing together traditional Indian craftsmanship and contemporary design sensibilities. Founded in 2005, we have grown from a small family workshop into one of the most trusted jewellery destinations in the region.

Every piece we create is a testament to our unwavering commitment to quality, purity, and artistry. Our master craftsmen, with decades of experience, breathe life into gold and diamonds, transforming them into wearable works of art that tell your unique story.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

2005

The Beginning

Shubhi Jewellers was established with a small collection of gold jewellery, serving our first loyal customers.

2009

Diamond Collection Launch

We expanded into diamond jewellery, partnering with certified diamond suppliers from Surat.

2014

Bridal Division

A dedicated bridal jewellery section was launched, offering complete wedding sets and trousseau consultations.

2018

Custom Jewellery Studio

State-of-the-art in-house design studio opened, offering fully bespoke jewellery creation services.

2023

10,000 Happy Families

A significant milestone — over 10,000 families now trust Shubhi Jewellers for their precious moments.
